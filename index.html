Straight answers. No sales-consultant fluff.

Tradesmen have the sharpest BS detectors in the country. We don't blame you. Let's look at the actual questions you care about—cost, hassle, and what happens if you hate it.

01 //

What does this cost? What’s the damage?

Transparent pricing, no hidden additions or upfront setup debt. Our packages start at $197/month and go to $397/month (most trade businesses settle on our mid-range package because it perfectly suits their needs). We also have add-on extras for Google reviews and local social media to keep your phone ringing. Zero lock-in contracts, cancel any time with buy out options available.

02 //

I already have a website. Why change?

If your current site brings in constant booked jobs and keeps your boys busy year-round, do not change it. Keep it as is. But most trade websites in Queensland are basically silent online brochures that do not do anything but cost money. This mockup layout is custom-tooled specifically to make your phone ring on-site. It loads instantly on mobile, and highlights exactly what homeowners look for—trust, credentials, and real speed. If your current site is sitting there doing sweet FA, let’s scrap it and swap it.

03 //

How long until we are live?

About 7 days. Because we have already put in the time to structure and research this mockup layout for you, the heavy lifting is complete. If you say yes, we just swap the generic parts with your actual work photos, type up your Queensland trade license numbers, lock in your exact pricing/guarantees, map your domain name, and hit the green light.

04 //

Do I have to do anything technical?

Zip. Absolutely none of it. We’ve worked with plenty of sparkies, builders, and drainers across southeast Queensland. We know you’d rather be on the tools or running your site than arguing with domains, servers, or DNS records. We handle the host, set up the SSL, test the contact logs, make sure it’s fast on standard mobile networks, and hand over a clean working product.

05 //

What if I don’t like the mockup?

Then we shake hands and walk away. That is why we build the initial draft first. It gives you a real tangible product to click around on and judge, rather than requiring you to buy a block of theoretical hours. If it does not fit your vibe, you haven’t spent a single brass razoo, and we keep the layout. No hard feelings, no high-pressure pushy followups.

06 //

Why is this so affordable? What’s the catch?

There is no catch. We don’t employ high-rise sales staff, we don’t book fancy corporate boardrooms in the Brisbane CBD, and we don't write lengthy 50-page strategy decks that nobody ever reads. We are a single focused engineering team that structures fast, hard-working websites for small trade operators. Our overhead is near zero, and we pass that efficiency straight to you. It is high end craftsmanship, priced like a couple of cartons of beer a month.
